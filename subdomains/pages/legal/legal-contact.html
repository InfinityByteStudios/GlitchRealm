6. Response Time Expectations

Expected response times by inquiry type:

  • Emergency (security breaches, DMCA): 24-48 hours
  • Privacy/GDPR Requests: 2 business days (legally required)
  • General Support: 1-3 business days
  • Developer Inquiries: 3-5 business days
  • Legal/Licensing: 5-7 business days

Response times may be longer during holidays or high-volume periods. Urgent matters should be clearly marked in the subject line.

7. Supervisory Authorities

For unresolved privacy or data protection complaints, you may contact the relevant supervisory authority:

  • Canada (PIPEDA): Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada
    Website: priv.gc.ca
    Phone: 1-800-282-1376
  • EU/EEA (GDPR): Your local Data Protection Authority (DPA)
    EU List: EDPB Members
  • California (CCPA): California Attorney General
    Website: oag.ca.gov/privacy
