GAMES & GAMEPLAY

Tips for playing and enjoying games on GlitchRealm

How to Launch Games

Click "Play Now" on any game card to launch the game in a new window or tab.

Playtime Tracking

Your playtime is automatically tracked and saved to your account for each game.

Game Controls

Each game has unique controls. Check the game's "How to Play" section for details.

Saving Progress

Some games auto-save your progress. Check individual game descriptions for save features.

Game Not Loading?

Troubleshoot loading issues: check your browser, internet connection, and ad blockers.

Reviewing Games

Share your thoughts! Leave reviews to help other players discover great games.
