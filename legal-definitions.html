Understanding Our Legal Terms

This glossary defines key legal terms used throughout GlitchRealm's policies and agreements. Understanding these definitions will help you better understand your rights and responsibilities when using our platform.

A-C D-I J-P Q-V W-Z

A - C

Account

Your registered user profile on GlitchRealm, including your username, email address, password, and associated data. Each user may maintain one account per person.

Agreement

The legally binding contract between you and GlitchRealm Games, consisting of our Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and any additional terms applicable to specific services.

Brand Names

The protected names, logos, and identifiers of GlitchRealm Games, including but not limited to "GlitchRealm" and "GlitchRealm Games", which may not be used without permission.

Community Guidelines

The rules and standards for acceptable behavior on GlitchRealm, as outlined in our Code of Conduct, designed to maintain a safe and respectful environment.

Content

Any information, data, text, graphics, images, audio, video, or other materials uploaded, posted, or transmitted through GlitchRealm services, including User-Generated Content.

D - I

Developer

An authorized member of the GlitchRealm development team with elevated privileges for platform maintenance and moderation. Developers are identified by specific UIDs in our system.

Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA)

U.S. copyright law that provides a mechanism for reporting copyright infringement. We comply with DMCA takedown procedures for reported violations.

Donation

A voluntary financial contribution to support GlitchRealm's development and operations. Donations are not purchases and do not entitle the donor to goods or services.

End User

Any person who accesses or uses any part of GlitchRealm's services or website. This includes users with registered accounts, anonymous guests, and anyone browsing or using features that don't require registration. In our legal documents, this is referred to as "you," "your," or "user."

Game Submission

A user-created game uploaded to GlitchRealm's platform for community access, subject to review and approval by moderators or developers.

Intellectual Property (IP)

Creations of the mind protected by law, including copyrights, trademarks, patents, and trade secrets. GlitchRealm's code is MIT licensed, while non-code assets are All Rights Reserved.

J - P

License

Legal permission to use intellectual property under specified conditions. GlitchRealm grants you a limited, non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use our services.

MIT License

A permissive open-source software license applied to GlitchRealm's source code, allowing use, modification, and distribution with minimal restrictions.

Moderator

A trusted community member or team member authorized to review content, enforce community guidelines, and take action against policy violations.

Personal Data

Information that identifies or can be used to identify you, including your name, email address, IP address, and browsing data. Protected under our Privacy Policy.

Platform

The GlitchRealm website, services, applications, games, and all associated infrastructure and systems provided by GlitchRealm Games.

Playtime

The duration a user actively plays a game on GlitchRealm, tracked and stored in our database for analytics and user statistics.

Q - V

Service

All features, functions, content, and applications provided by GlitchRealm, including game hosting, user authentication, community features, and related offerings.

Third-Party Services

External services integrated with GlitchRealm, such as Firebase (authentication), Supabase (storage), and Netlify (hosting). These services have their own terms and privacy policies.

Terms of Service (ToS)

The legally binding agreement governing your use of GlitchRealm's platform. By using our services, you agree to be bound by these terms.

User-Generated Content (UGC)

Content created and submitted by users, including game submissions, reviews, community posts, and comments. You retain ownership but grant GlitchRealm a license to use it.

Verified User

A user who has been granted a verified creator badge by GlitchRealm developers, indicating trusted status within the community.

Virtual Items

Digital items, currency, or rewards within GlitchRealm games that have no cash value and are non-transferable outside the platform.

W - Z

We, Us, Our

Refers to GlitchRealm Games, the entity that owns and operates the GlitchRealm platform.

Website

The GlitchRealm website accessible at glitchrealm.ca, glitchrealm.netlify.app, and all associated subdomains and pages.

You, Your

Refers to the End User accessing or using GlitchRealm's services, whether as a registered user or guest.
