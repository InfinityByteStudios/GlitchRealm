1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide functionality, remember your preferences, analyze usage patterns, and improve your experience on GlitchRealm.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential/Functional Authentication (Firebase Auth), session management, user preferences Persistent (until logout or cleared)
Analytics Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics - page views, user behavior, performance metrics Up to 2 years
Third-Party Services Supabase storage, Firebase services, CDN providers Varies by provider

3. Tracking Technologies

In addition to cookies, we use the following tracking technologies:

  • Local Storage: Stores Firebase authentication tokens, user preferences, game progress, and playtime data locally in your browser
  • Session Storage: Temporary data storage during your browsing session
  • Firebase Analytics: Tracks events like game launches, playtime, page views, and user interactions
  • Google Analytics: Monitors site performance, traffic sources, and user demographics
  • CDN Analytics: Netlify, gstatic.com, and other CDN providers may collect performance metrics

4. Data Collected Through Cookies

Cookies and tracking technologies may collect:

  • User ID and authentication tokens (Firebase)
  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type, operating system, screen resolution
  • Pages visited, referral sources, time spent on pages
  • Game interactions, playtime duration, achievements
  • User preferences (theme, language, accessibility settings)
  • Error logs and diagnostic data

5. Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services that may set cookies on your device:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, Firestore database, Analytics, Cloud Functions
  • Supabase: Image storage and avatar hosting
  • Google Fonts: Web font delivery
  • Netlify: Hosting and CDN services
  • jsDelivr CDN: Library and asset delivery

These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies. We do not control third-party cookies.

6. Cookie Consent

By using GlitchRealm, you consent to the use of cookies and tracking technologies as described in this policy. If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or California, you have additional rights regarding cookie consent and data processing.

We use a Consent Management Platform (CMP) to manage your cookie preferences for analytics and non-essential cookies. Essential cookies required for site functionality cannot be disabled.

7. How to Manage Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings:

Browser Controls

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Cookies and site permissions

Opt-Out of Analytics

  • Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Firebase Analytics Privacy Controls

Note: Disabling essential cookies may prevent you from accessing certain features, including authentication, game saves, and personalized content.

8. Do Not Track Signals

GlitchRealm does not currently respond to "Do Not Track" (DNT) browser signals. However, you can manage cookies and tracking through the methods described above.

9. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect cookies or tracking data from users under 13 years of age without verifiable parental consent. See our Children's Privacy Policy for details.
